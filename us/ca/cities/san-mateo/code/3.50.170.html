City of San Mateo Municipal Code.

3.50.170 TERMINATION DATE.

The authority to levy the tax imposed by this chapter shall expire on March 31, 2048.

(Ord. No. 2015-11 § 1; Ord. No. 2010-6 § 1; Ord. No. 2010-1 § 1.)
